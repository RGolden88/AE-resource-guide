Top 20 Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Atlas Earth?

Atlas Earth is a mobile game where players buy virtual land parcels and earn small amounts of rent over time.

2. What are Atlas Bucks?

Atlas Bucks, also called AB, are the in-game currency used to buy parcels, badges, and other game items.

3. What are parcels?

Parcels are virtual pieces of land. Each parcel has a rarity that affects how much rent it earns.

4. What are the parcel rarities?

The main parcel rarities are common, rare, epic, and legendary.

5. What is a legendary parcel?

A legendary parcel is the highest normal parcel rarity and earns more than common, rare, or epic parcels.

6. What is an LPU?

LPU means Legendary Parcel Upgrade. It makes the parcel it is placed on earn like a legendary parcel.

7. What are badges?

Badges are collectible location items that can increase your rent bonus when you reach certain badge tiers.

8. Should I buy badges or parcels first?

Most beginners focus on parcels first, then buy badges when a badge bonus tier becomes useful.

9. What is boosting?

Boosting means watching ads to multiply your rent earnings for a set amount of time.

10. What is SRB?

SRB means Super Rent Boost. During SRB events, rent receives a much higher temporary boost.

11. What is tier jumping?

Tier jumping means buying enough parcels to move from one boost multiplier tier to another.

12. Why does my boost multiplier drop?

In some countries, owning more parcels can move you into a lower regular boost multiplier tier.

13. What are diamonds used for?

Diamonds are used for daily wheel spins, which can reward Atlas Bucks and other prizes.

14. How many diamond spins can I do per day?

Players can usually do a limited number of spins per day depending on the current game rules.

15. What are minigames?

Minigames are special events like racing or fishing where players compete for leaderboard rewards.

16. What does leaderboard mean?

The leaderboard shows player rankings during events or competitions.

17. What does “the line” mean?

The line is the last paying spot in a leaderboard bracket, such as 25th, 50th, 100th, or 500th place.

18. Why are my ads not loading?

Try restarting the app, checking your internet connection, turning off VPNs, or waiting a few minutes before trying again.

19. Can I make a lot of money with Atlas Earth?

Atlas Earth is usually slow earning. Beginners should treat it like a game, not a reliable income source.
