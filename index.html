Atlas Earth was officially launched in November 2021. Developed by Atlas Reality, Inc.

Atlas Earth is a location-based meta-universe app where you can purchase real-world parcels in a virtual landscape for real money!

Yes, I said real money. No, it’s not a scam! I have personally cashed out several times. However, Atlas Earth has the unfortunate reputation of being a “scam” site because some people decided to put money into this app without looking into it. This guide will help you navigate the pitfalls so that you will not waste your time or money on this app.

What are the cash out options?

Paypal, Venmo, CashApp, gift card, ACH to bank account, and donations. There is a $5 minimum and amounts must be in whole numbers.

Start Learning

Parcels

Parcels are the core of Atlas Earth. Each parcel you own earns rent over time and can be Common, Rare, Epic, or Legendary. As your parcel count grows, so does your earning potential, making parcels the foundation of every successful Atlas Earth account.

Badges

Badges provide a rent bonus in Atlas Earth. Purchased from real-world locations, badges increase your earnings as you reach higher badge tiers. While parcels are usually the first priority, strategically purchasing badges can significantly boost your long-term rent income.

Boosts

Boosts multiply your rent earnings for a set amount of time. Keeping your boost active as often as possible is one of the best ways to maximize your Atlas Earth income.
