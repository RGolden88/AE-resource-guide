What is the Challenge Ladder?

The Atlas Earth Challenge Ladder is a monthly points system that begins at midnight on the first day of each month and runs through the last day of the month. Players earn points by completing challenges such as boosting, buying parcels, collecting badges, playing minigames, completing surveys, and more.

The goal is to reach 1,600 points before the month ends. Players can choose to complete as many or as few challenges as they want. Completing the ladder rewards players with Atlas Bucks, a badge, and bid tokens. Subscribers may also receive a Legendary Parcel Upgrade (LPU) as part of the reward track.
