1. What is Atlas Earth?

Atlas Earth is a mobile game where players collect virtual land parcels and earn small amounts of rent over time.

2. Parcel Basics

Parcels can have different rarities. Higher rarity parcels usually earn more than common parcels.

  • Common
  • Rare
  • Epic
  • Legendary

3. Boosting

Boosting is one of the most important beginner habits. Many players try to keep their boost active as much as possible.

4. Badges

Badges can provide bonus rent. A simple beginner strategy is to learn when it makes sense to buy parcels and when it makes sense to buy badges.

There is some terms you will see just starting out you are not going to understand. I know I didn't. This list helps.

Acronyms

AB – Atlas Bucks

AE – Atlas Earth

AMP – Atlas Earth Merchant Program

B – Added to the end of a number to indicate how many badges are owned. For example, 11B means “11 badges”.

EC – Explorer’s Club; sometimes appears as AEC for Atlas Explorer’s Club

LB – LeaderBoard

LM – LandMark

LPU – Legendary Parcel Upgrade

MG – MiniGame

MM – MatchMaking

P – Added to the end of a number to indicate how many parcels are owned. For example, 110P means “110 parcels”.

PFP – proFile Picture

T – Added after a number to indicate the payable bracket on the minigame. For example, T25 means “top 25”.

SRB – Super Rent Boost

SP – Season Pass (for challenges)

F2P - Free to Play

Beginner Tip

Do not spend real money unless you understand the game and are comfortable with the slow earning pace.
